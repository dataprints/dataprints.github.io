Project Objectives

The Food Assistance Sub Project aims to provide nutritious meals to the children at the VEROWGDEC orphanage school, ensuring their health and well-being. The project seeks to:

  • Provide daily meals to children, ensuring they receive the necessary nutrients for growth and development
  • Improve the overall health and well-being of children by addressing malnutrition and food insecurity
  • Enhance the learning environment by ensuring children are well-nourished and able to focus on their studies
  • Foster a sense of community and support among children through shared meals
  • Raise awareness about the importance of nutrition and healthy eating habits
  • Engage local communities and stakeholders in supporting the food assistance program
  • Monitor and evaluate the impact of the food assistance program on children's health and academic performance
  • Promote sustainable practices in food sourcing and preparation
  • Collaborate with local farmers and suppliers to source fresh and nutritious food
