Sharing a meal

At Ddembelyabato orphanage project, we believe that sharing a meal is one of the most fundamental ways to show love and care. We ensure that all 82 children currently at our premises receive a balanced meal every day. This not only nourishes their bodies but also creates a sense of community and belonging among them.

Free Education

At Ddembelyabato orphanage project, we are committed to providing free education to all the children in our care. We believe that education is a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty and empowering the next generation. Our educational programs range from baby class to primary seven, ensuring that every child has access to quality learning opportunities.

Spritual Guidance

At Ddembelyabato orphanage project, we understand the importance of spiritual growth. We encourage our children to read the Bible and engage in regular prayer. Our partnership with Siloam Pool Ministries allows us to provide spiritual guidance and support, helping the children develop a strong moral foundation and a sense of purpose.

Free Shelter

At Ddembelyabato orphanage project, we provide a safe and nurturing environment for all the children in our care. Our premises offer free shelter, ensuring that every child has a place to call home. We believe that a stable living environment is essential for the emotional and physical well-being of the children, allowing them to thrive and grow.
