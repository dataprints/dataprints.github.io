Project Objectives

The Scholarship Sub Project aims to provide educational opportunities for children at the VEROWGDEC orphanage school, particularly those who have dropped out of secondary school due to financial constraints. The project seeks to:

  • Provide financial support for school fees, uniforms, and educational materials
  • Encourage children to return to school and complete their education
  • Empower children with knowledge and skills for a better future
  • Foster a culture of education and learning within the community
  • Support the holistic development of children, including their academic, social, and emotional well-being
  • Collaborate with local schools and educational institutions to ensure quality education
  • Raise awareness about the importance of education and the challenges faced by orphaned children
  • Engage the community and stakeholders in supporting the education of orphaned children
  • Monitor and evaluate the impact of the scholarship program on children's education and well-being
